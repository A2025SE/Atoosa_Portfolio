About me


My name is Atoosa Falsafi, and I am currently employed as a news graphic designer at SVT, where I work on various TV programs and news broadcasts aimed at different target audiences. I also have experience working with intranets, maintaining direct communication with clients, and understanding how different editorial teams collaborate to produce programs. I studied the IT, Media, and Design program at Södertörn University, which focused on the creation of digital content, graphic design, UX design, and web design.

I am highly engaged and passionate about creating images, videos, websites, and text. I have strong skills in Adobe programs such as Photoshop, Premiere Pro, InDesign, and Illustrator, as well as in WordPress, HTML, and CSS. During my studies, I have also gained advanced knowledge in image and video editing, as well as web development.

Graphic Design Work at SVT

Graphic Segment on Biogas


This is a graphic I designed for a segment on biogas featured in one of SVT's local news broadcasts. The segment includes animations of various icons and symbols. The graphic segment was produced using the editing software Adobe After Effects.

Graphic Segment on Electricity Prices


This is a graphic I created for a segment on electricity prices, broadcast in SVT's news program Rapport. The graphic features a line chart animated using various selected effects. Its purpose is to visualize electricity prices over a two-year period. The graphic segment was produced using the editing software Adobe After Effects.

Graphic Segment on Northvolt


This is a graphic I created for a segment on Northvolt's debts, broadcast in SVT's news program Rapport. The graphic consists of several elements animated using various selected effects. Its purpose is to visualize three different invoices with varying amounts. The graphic segment was produced using the editing software Adobe After Effects.

Graphic Segment on Emissions


This is a graphic I created for a segment on Northvolt's debts, broadcast in SVT's news programs Rapport and Morgonstudion. The graphic consists of several text headings animated using various selected effects. Its purpose is to visualize the specific increase in CO2 for the years 2023–2024. The graphic segment was produced using the editing software Adobe After Effects.

Graphic Segment on Wind Power


This is a graphic I created for a segment on wind turbines in Sweden, broadcast in SVT's news programs Rapport, Aktuellt, Morgonstudion, and some of SVT's local news broadcasts. The graphic consists of a map of Sweden highlighting wind farms, showing which ones will not be stopped by the government. The graphic segment was produced using the editing software Adobe After Effects.

Graphic Segment on Preschool Children


This is a graphic I created for a segment on preschool children's absenteeism, broadcast in SVT's news program Rapport. The graphic consists of a bar chart that is animated and divided into six age groups. For clarity and to make it easier for viewers to follow and understand the graphic, three color boxes were also created to explain what each bar color represents. The graphic segment was produced using the editing software Adobe After Effects.
